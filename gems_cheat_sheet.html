Reference

XP Penalty Ranges

XP falls off if you are too far below a zone level.

  • 1-15: 3 levels under.
  • 16-32: 4 levels under.
  • 33-48: 5 levels under.
  • 49+: 6 levels under.

Weapon Damage Roll Reference

If the DMG roll is NOT within two tiers of your highest attainable, it's not worth crafting on. Hold alt to view item tiers.

Recommended Spear bases to use currency on:

  • Lvl 16 (Winged Spear, atk speed % for Whirling slash)
  • Lvl 21 (War Spear)
  • Lvl 45 (Crossblade Spear)
  • Lvl 65 (Helix Spear)

General Tips

  1. Hug zone walls to consistently find objectives and exits.
  2. Keep Currency in dedicated locations on left. Place junk on right.
  3. Buy plenty of wisdoms.
  4. Throw out old / useless gems/currency.
  5. If knocked down in sprint, log out to character screen to get up instantly.
  6. Ctrl + left click on Hooded One to instant ID (also to move quest items).
