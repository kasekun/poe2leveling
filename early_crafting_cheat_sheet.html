2. Early Crafting Materials

Blue Currencies

  • Transmutations & Augmentations: Spam on every good base: make it magic, get two stats.
  • Regal Orbs: Upgrade magic → rare only when both mods are already desirable.

Essences

  • Sorcery & Battle: +2 skills to wands/staffs and martial weapons. Only on bases with high existing spell or phys damage.
  • Thawing / Insulation / Electricity / Ruin: cold / fire / lightning / chaos res. Only on belts with high life/res, or boots with 30% move speed.

Putrifactions (Omen of Putrifaction)

6 unrevealed mods, 3 choices each. No Exalted Orbs needed. Add sockets and quality before using.

  • Preserved Rib: Armor pieces: best on Boots, Body Armors, Gloves.
  • Preserved Collarbone: Jewelry: best on Rings and Quivers.
  • Avoid: Preserved Jawbones (Weapons) or any putrifaction on Belts: results are historically terrible.

4. Mod Modifier Hierarchy

  • Priority 1: Exclusive mods: Base-specific power first: Move Speed on boots, +Skills on amulets.
  • Priority 2: Resistances: Any roll ≥30% is acceptable for early mapping.
  • Priority 3: Defenses: Match base type: Armor (STR) / Evasion (DEX) / Energy Shield (INT).
  • Wands & Staffs: High base spell damage + +2 to skills.
  • Martial Weapons: High base phys damage + max flat rolls (T11+) + +2 to all attack skills.
